# Avatar Illustration Standard

Approved direction for Mụta Igbo and the future MekaOps Edu Illustration Engine.

- Premium 3D cartoon Igbo kid faces
- Transparent/backgroundless PNGs
- No embedded name labels in the asset
- Names in the app remain product/content data, not burned into images
- Avatar art can be reused across MekaOps Edu products
- Use app UI to frame/crop avatars, not the source image
