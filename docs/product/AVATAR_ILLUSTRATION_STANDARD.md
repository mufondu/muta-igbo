## Rendering Rule

- Use one avatar per file.
- Do not use checkerboard/grid source sheets in app.
- Do not hardcode retired names.
- Do not crop avatars with cover.
- Use resizeMode contain.
- Use transparent wrappers.
- Let the app card own the background.
