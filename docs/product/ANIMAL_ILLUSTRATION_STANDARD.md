# Animal Illustration Standard

This is the approved animal illustration direction for Mụta Igbo and the future MekaOps Edu Illustration Engine.

## Approved Production Style

- Premium 3D cartoon animal
- Clean white or transparent background
- No cream mini-card background
- No border
- No embedded text
- No emoji fallback
- Centered animal subject
- Safe padding so the animal is not cut off
- Designed to blend into white lesson cards
