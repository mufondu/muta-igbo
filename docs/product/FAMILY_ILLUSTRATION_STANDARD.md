Approved art direction for family illustrations:

- Semi-realistic children’s book portrait style
- Igbo/African attire with warm cultural cues
- Rounded-square composition
- Friendly facial expression
- Soft home or village-inspired background
- Readable at 74px in lesson cards
- No emoji or procedural placeholder portraits as final art
